Wen Lai (赖文)

I am a final-year PhD student in School of Computation, Information and Technology at Technical University of Munich (TUM) under the supervision of Prof. Alexander Fraser and Prof. Ivan Titov. I am affiliated with and supported by the Munich Center for Machine Learning. Previously, I was a PhD student in Center for Information and Language Processing (CIS) at LMU Munich from April 2021 to March 2024.

Email Google Scholar Github Tweet Linkedin Blog

Research Interests

  • Machine Translation: Low-resource MT, Domain Adaptation, Multilingual MT, LLM for MT
  • Machine Learning: Meta-Learning, Transfer Learning, Contrastive Learning
  • Multimodal: Large Vision-Language Models
